ISA:

16-bit instructions

===== Type R instructions ====
 ____________________
|xxxx|sss|ttt|ddd|000|
 ‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾

ADD: 

Assembly: ADD $rd,$rs,$rt
Equivalent: $rd = $rs + $rt
Encoding: 0000 ssst ttdd d000

===== Type I instructions ====
 ___________________
|xxxx|sss|ttt|iiiiii|
 ‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾‾

ADDI: 

Assembly: ADDI $rt,$rs,Imm
Equivalent: $rt = $rs + Imm
Encoding: 1000 ssst ttii iiii
